Pet Camera Night Vision: Infrared vs. Color — Which to Pick

How IR and full-color night vision differ, and which suits a pet owner.

Night vision lets you check on a pet in a dark room. Infrared is invisible but black-and-white; color needs a light but shows detail.

Infrared (IR)

Invisible to pets, black-and-white, no light disturbance. Best for not waking a sleeping pet.

Color night vision

Uses a dim warm light to show color; better for identifying what your pet is chewing.

IR range

Check the stated range; cheap cameras wash out past a few feet.
