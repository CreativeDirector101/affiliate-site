Automatic Dog Ball Launcher: Tire Out Your Dog Hands-Free

Automatic ball launchers compared on safety, distance settings, size and indoor use.

An automatic launcher gives high-energy dogs a workout when you're busy. The priority is safety and the right ball size for your dog's mouth.

Ball size = safety

Only use the launcher's matched balls. A too-small ball is a choking risk for big dogs; a too-big one jams the mechanism.

Distance & indoor use

Look for multiple distance settings; the shortest should be safe indoors. Some have a 'scan' safety pause if your dog stands in front.

Power options

Battery for yard use, plug-in for indoor. Check which your layout needs.
