Best Smart / Microchip Pet Door: Let Only Your Pet In

Microchip and collar-tag smart pet doors compared on compatibility, security and installation.

A smart pet door ends the 'let the dog out at 6am' routine and keeps raccoons out. The key is matching the door to your pet's microchip or tag.

Microchip vs. collar tag

Microchip-reading doors never get 'lost' like a collar tag can, but only work if your pet is chipped. Collar-tag doors are cheaper and work for any pet but fail if the collar comes off.

Security & intruders

A smart door that reads your pet's chip won't open for the neighbor's cat or a raccoon. Check the locking modes (in-only, out-only, full, locked).

Installation reality

Most mount in a standard door or a wall. Wall installs need a tunnel kit. Measure your pet's shoulder height — mount the flap so they don't trip.

Frequently Asked Questions

Will it work with any microchip?
Most read the common 9, 10 and 15-digit ISO chips, but confirm your pet's chip type against the door spec.
Can I keep my pet in at night?
Yes — all smart doors have a manual lock mode for nighttime or weather.
