Using a Baby Monitor as a Pet Camera (Cheap Alternative)

How a spare baby monitor can double as a pet cam — pros, cons and privacy.

Baby monitors are a low-cost way to watch a pet, often with no subscription and strong night vision. They trade pet-specific apps for simplicity.

Pros

No monthly fee, great night vision, often two-way audio, local or FHSS (private) signals.

Cons

No pet alerts, no treat toss, limited app features, range limits.

Privacy

FHSS monitors don't go online — strongest privacy; Wi-Fi baby cams do.
